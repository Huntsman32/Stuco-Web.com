ABOUT THE COMPANY

Key features of our company

Professional Ironing

The service offers professional ironing performed by skilled and experienced personnel who are trained in handling various types of fabrics.

OUR WORK

What we've done for people

Receive and Sort Garments: Establish a system to receive garments from customers and sort them based on fabric type, care instructions, and any special requirements. This helps ensure that each garment receives appropriate treatment during the ironing process.

Pre-Treatment and Inspection: Check each garment for stains, loose buttons, or other issues that may require special attention before ironing. Pre-treat any stain or address any necessary repairs or adjustments.

Ironing Techniques and Equipment: Determine the appropriate ironing techniques and equipment for different fabric types. This may include selecting the appropriate heat settings, using steam or dry ironing methods, and employing specialized tools such as pressing cloths or steamers.

Ironing Process: Develop a standardized ironing process that includes the sequence of garments, ironing techniques, and any additional steps required. Consider factors such as ironing temperature, ironing duration, and the order in which garments should be ironed to maximize efficiency.

Quality Control: Implement quality control measures to ensure that each garment meets the desired standards. This may involve checking for creases, proper folding, and overall appearance. Consider having a checklist or quality control checklist to ensure consistency.

Packaging and Delivery: Determine how the ironed garments will be packaged and prepared for return to the customers. This may involve using garment covers, hangers, or folding techniques to maintain the garments' quality during transportation. Establish an efficient system for delivering the ironed garments back to the customers.

Customer Communication: Develop clear communication channels with customers to manage their expectations, provide updates on service progress, and address any concerns or special requests they may have. This can be done through various channels such as phone, email, or a dedicated online platform.

Record-Keeping: Maintain accurate records of customer orders, service details, and any specific customer preferences. This helps in providing personalized service in the future and streamlining the operational processes.
